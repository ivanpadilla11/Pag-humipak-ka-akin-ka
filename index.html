Dota Pacific Coaching

Welcome to our website, where we are on a mission to provide exceptional coaching services for the beginners and stuck up players in low rank. As a startup, we believe that MMR is just a number however we seen a lot of players struggles to escape the low elo rank. I know it's stressful but the DPC team will guide you to climb to your desire rank in a enjoyable way.

Why to choose DPC?

Professional

Our team of professional Dota Player are trained to compete well known tournament like TI (The International). While they are waiting for the next Tournament they decided to help other players who struggles to escape their ranks by coaching them.
